About Me

My passion for art and design has deeply influenced my entire life and continues to motivate me as a multi-media designer. Over 10 years of design experience in academic and professional fields helped me develop a sharp instinct for design by translating conceptual ideas into compelling visual design, delivering something that not only pleased the user but delights them.

Experienced collaborating with cross-functional teams and managing projects from corporations across the countries in different industries. I am keen to be open-minded to people from different backgrounds and cultures.

My Work

Personal Branding Design

The design defines my core values, skills, and personality qualities through hitting the keywords in my portfolio identification project, which include boldness, charm, and meticulous. I chose blue, gray, black, and orange as theme colors since they reflect my personality and the emotions I want to provoke. In a psychological sense, the majority usage of the blue hue represents calm, cleanliness, and harmony. The design output maintains a consistent appearance across different scenarios in everyday living. This includes materials such as my business card, promotion posters, stationery, and so on.The design created continuous visual recognition and helped to develop my own brand.
